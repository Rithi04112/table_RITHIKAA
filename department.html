Department of Information technology

Information technology(IT) is the use of computers to create,process,store,retrieve and exchange all kinds of electronic data and information.It is typically used within the context of business operations as oppossed to personal informations. It is considered to be the subset of information and communications technology(ICT).An information technology system(IT system) is generally an information system,a communication system or more specifically a computer system.Information technology is also a branch of computer science ,which can be defined as the overall study of procedure,structure and the processing of various type of data.The field of information technology(IT) covers the design,administration and support of computer and telecommunications systems.Some of the positions in this field include database and network administrators,computer support specialists,computer scientists,software programmers and system analysts.The majority of career tracks in IT entail design and operational tasks related to computer hardware componenets,networks and software applications.


Proffessionals in the IT field work with businesses and organizations to setup and support viable computer networks that will keep systems efficient and reliable .IT encompasses all hardware and software used in the storing,creation and accessing information.Examples of technologies that professionals work with are

  • Firewalls
  • Database
  • Media storage devices
  • Network and internet

Aspiring IT professionals typically need a bachelor's degree in a technological field,such as

  1. Computer science
  2. Information system management
  3. Programming or networking
.The fundamentals of a degree program include computer hardware,programming,networking technical theory.Some programs may also include intenship opportunities.
